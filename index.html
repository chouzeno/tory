ABOUT TORY

TORY International Co., Ltd. was established in September 2004, with its headquarters located in Taipei, Taiwan. An overseas office in Suzhou was opened in 2016 to expand international services.

How can we help you?

TORY not only represents major Korean manufacturers such as MCS (Sockets) and MSL (Conductive Adhesives) but also provides customized testing JIGs and high-power electronic components based on customers' IC/PCB requirements.

Why US?
