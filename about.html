Company Profile:

TORY International Co., Ltd. was established in September 2004, with its headquarters located in Taipei, Taiwan. An overseas office in Suzhou was opened in 2016 to expand international services. TORY not only represents major Korean manufacturers such as MCS (Sockets) and MSL (Conductive Adhesives) but also provides customized testing JIGs and high-power electronic components based on customers' IC/PCB requirements. With 20 years of accumulated experience and a customer-first approach, TORY has earned the trust and partnership of numerous clients. Our customer base includes publicly traded IC design companies and semiconductor packaging and testing factories, both domestically and internationally. We are an indispensable partner within the semiconductor supply chain.

Semiconductor Industry Partner

As Taiwan's semiconductor industry took off, TORY International Co., Ltd. focused on developing the market for backend testing equipment in the semiconductor packaging and testing sector. We specialize in the research, manufacturing, and sales of testing components and have independently developed patented IC test sockets. Our expertise lies in the global procurement, sales, and integration of testing equipment. Over the years, we have collaborated with world-renowned companies such as Motorola, Broadcom, Cypress, Avago, Synaptics, Foxconn, Infineon, Texas Instruments, Quanta, and Flextronics, building a strong portfolio of experience. With over 20 years of expertise in the electronics and technology industry, we are well-versed not only in the semiconductor supply chain but also in PC, notebook, mobile, and memory sectors, having worked with more than 1,000 suppliers worldwide. At the peak of our business, we strategically shifted our focus to international trade, with a primary presence across the Asia-Pacific region, including Taiwan, China, South Korea, Japan, and the United States. With strong relationships within the industry and 20 years of business operations in Taiwan, our future focus is expanding into the Americas and South America, with a particular emphasis on the AI industry. We are committed to reaching new heights and driving the next phase of growth for TORY International.
